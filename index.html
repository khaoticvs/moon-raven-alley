Welcome to Moon Raven Alley!

Moon Raven Alley is a local metaphysical shop located at

65C E. Main Street in Mount Joy, Pennsylvania. Rooted in a

rich history, diverse culture, small town atmosphere.

Surrounded by charming streets, and neighboring

communities worth exploring, our location places you at

the heart of Lancaster County.

We are located just minutes from:

  • Downtown Lancaster City: 20 minutes
  • Elizabethtown, 15 minutes
  • Hershey, 30 minutes
  • Marietta, 10 minutes
  • Columbia, 15 minutes
  • and more...

The building itself carries decades of memories. Once part

of the Historic Joy Theater complex (c.1938-1965),

the storefront, still reflects its early 20th- century

Queen-Anne style architectural influence (c. 1920). Though

its purpose has evolved, the space continues to offer the

same quiet, welcoming atmosphere that once invited

generations through its doors.

    Situated right on main street, Moon Raven Alley stands

    among the buildings that helped shape Mount Joy's

    Historic district which was once a central hub of

    community life and connection.
